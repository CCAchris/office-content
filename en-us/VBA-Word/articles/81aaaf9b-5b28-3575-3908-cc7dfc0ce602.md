
# TextColumns.Width Property (Word)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Returns or sets the width of the Word art text effects, in points. Read/write  **Long**.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **Width**

 _expression_A variable that represents a  ** [TextColumns](00b62c93-db7d-00b9-cc84-9a21e427d0cd.md)** collection.
