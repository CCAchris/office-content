
# FormField.Delete Method (Word)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Deletes the specified form field.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **Delete**

 _expression_Required. A variable that represents a  ** [FormField](c3c07344-06b2-fe86-6fcb-b9c63a991bcc.md)** object.
