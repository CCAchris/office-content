
# Point.Delete Method (Word)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Deletes the object.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **Delete**

 _expression_A variable that represents a  ** [Point](349ea9a3-9e9a-b26f-146f-799d39c3d4a9.md)** object.


## Remarks

Deleting a  **Point** object deletes the entire series.
