
# AddIn.Delete Method (Word)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Deletes the specified add-in.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **Delete**

 _expression_Required. A variable that represents an  ** [AddIn](5615a8a9-1fd6-04fa-1fee-ec16502bd84a.md)** object.
