
# XlPivotFieldOrientation Enumeration (Excel)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Specifies the location of the field in a PivotTable report.


|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlColumnField**|2|Column|
| **xlDataField**|4|Data|
| **xlHidden**|0|Hidden|
| **xlPageField**|3|Page|
| **xlRowField**|1|Row|
