
# XlDirection Enumeration (Excel)

 **Last modified:** March 09, 2015

Specifies the direction in which to move.

## Version Information

Version Added: Excel 2007 



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlDown**|-4121|Down.|
| **xlToLeft**|-4159|To left.|
| **xlToRight**|-4161|To right.|
| **xlUp**|-4162|Up.|
