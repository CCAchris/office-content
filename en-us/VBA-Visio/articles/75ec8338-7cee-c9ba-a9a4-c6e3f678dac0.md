
# VisRoundFlags Enumeration (Visio)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

 _**Applies to:** Visio 2013 Preview_

|**Constant**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **visRound**|1|Round the result.|
| **visTruncate**|0|Truncate the result.|
