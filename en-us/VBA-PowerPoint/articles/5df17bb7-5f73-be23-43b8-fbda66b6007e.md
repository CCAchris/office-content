
# Point.Delete Method (PowerPoint)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Deletes the object.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **Delete**

 _expression_A variable that represents a  ** [Point](e0137fdd-5632-88d7-a6c0-57a76717e736.md)** object.


## Remarks

Deleting a  **Point** object deletes the entire series.
